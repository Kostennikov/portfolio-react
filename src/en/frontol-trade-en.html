Frontol Trade — Windows app for inventory system

Frontol Trade is an app for those, who are just starting to keep their inventory records. In essence it’s a set of electronic documents, that simplify work with goods and suppliers. The app is an add-on to Frontol Simple cash register.

The main task of the program is to know what is sold in the store and to help edit a lot of documents and products in parallel.

All scenarios are cross-referenced. For example, while in a document, the user can add a new item or a supplier and then go back in one click.

Or switch to a balance report.

Design of all scenarios fit into a hundred screens.

Made in 2017 in 4 weeks
