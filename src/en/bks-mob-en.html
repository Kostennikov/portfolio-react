Mobile service for partners of BCS Capital

BCS Capital "Partner" is a partnership service for selling investment products. I’ve designed its mobile version based on BCS design system.

Transactions

The main mission of the mobile version is to sell products to new clients and quickly respond to errors in transactions. Managers need to mess around with clients’ documents a lot during sells. Unfortunately the system has not been yet taught to scan passports and fill in forms, so managers do it manually. To make their lifes easier, we created a search by passport ID, and if the client is already in the system, the form automatically filles up.

During the process it’s often needed to move to another task, for example, to open a brokerage account. The system interface allows it.

Taking care of managers

Hints about errors in transactions contribute to their rapid elimination and preserve KPI. "My Clients" page shows what else can be offered to a client. Besides it’s not necessary to have a big screen smartphone to enjoy comfort, because the interface looks and feels good on small screens as well.

Training

There is a lot of qualification tests and useful information in your personal account. The system saves the progress so the tests can be run intermittently.
