‘Olivier’
portal logo

‘Olivier’ is a portal for shops owners, invented for promoting ATOL products. I’ve helped to come up with the name, designed the logo and a website.

The name and logo highlight, that business consist of different elements, whose qualities define success. In it, as in olivier, it is important to strike a balance between the ‘ingredients’, rather than blindly pour out a bucket of mayonnaise.

Illustrations

In the main version of logo I drew money for outright declaration that the portal first and foremost is all about how to impact on profit.

But money is not the only important thing, so the logo quickly transforms into illustrations with different ‘ingredients’.
