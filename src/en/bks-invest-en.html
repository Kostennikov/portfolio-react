Investment reports for BCS company clients
Context

In BCS application you can request a brief or detailed report on your investment portfolio, specifying the period and the currency. The system will analyze the history and output a PDF file of several pages. This solution doesn't requre adaptation to the layout of different sites and applications.

This is what the report looked like before the redesign

You can scroll here

Sloppy layout, unobvious and illogocal structure, unnecessary and at same time insufficient information that even experienced investors have troubles to read. One more problem is that the data structure of the report didn't match the information in the app or the website.

Therefore, first of all I figured out the information order, its grouping and then the design.

The result

All reports are built from general to specific. The first page shows an overall summary for each investment portfolio, which immidiately shows the whole picture.

The report is arranged in blocks, so the lack of any information is not terrible.

Exploring old reports showed that the information in the tables is simpler than it seems at first glance. All that I needed to do was to remove all the excess and carefully redesign it.

Before the redesign, all the helpful information was mixed with numbers, that distracted reading. In the new report the numbers are always in the foreground.

I paid special attention to the product pages. They show key features, graphs and useful information.

Some charts show not only the dynamics of profitability, but also suggest how the product works. This graph shows how the lower threshold is falling: the further away the more likely it is to receive a coupon payment.
