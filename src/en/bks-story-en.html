BCS narratives about investment types

Structural products are investments based on stocks of large companies. In a successful scenario, the investor receives payments every few months and at the end of the envestment period returns the invested money plus interest. And in case of failure, as a rule, the protection of the invested capital is triggered.

Different protection and profit mechanics were invented for different types of products. I figured out how to explain them clearly and designed interactive learning.

Start page

All products have different profit conditions. But they have a common feature: the result of the investment depends on the position of the asset in the market. Therefore, the narrative is built around the movement of the asset. Scenarios and design elements are inextricably connected with text, graphics and animation.

Examples

For clarity examples have been invented that are close to reality. The asset is rapidly "living" the entire term of the investment, getting into a variety of situations.
