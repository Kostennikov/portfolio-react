Kassa

1C:Mobilnaya Kassa (Mobile Cash Register) — is an adroid app for cashiers, couriers and managers. It helps to automate a small store with retail and deliverу.

The main thing — without training

In order to make cashiers and couriers lifes easier, it was important to create an interface that doesn’t require experience with similar apps. Functions on homescreen clearly speak for themselves and the app leads through every process by the hand.

Sales process

Work with orders

The app allows to create orders in the interface or upload from 1C:Kassa app, receive payment, prepayment and postpayment. Working with orders is made to be similar to a regular sale — it makes development and adaptation essentially much easier.
