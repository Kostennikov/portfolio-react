Android app for goods accounting automatization

Cleverence is one of the largest developers of goods accounting automatization software. Vova Lavrentiev and I redesigned the application for warehouse work and put together a design system, so that developers could assemble new pages without designers.

Main screen

When redesigning the main screen it was important to save the "rainbow", because users got used to it. But competitors managed to peek at this solution and do the same. Therefore, it was necessery to come up with a design, that would stand out and not break habits.


Before → After:

Systematization

It was important to help developers assemble new screens, that are being released almost every week. They spent a lot of time on coming up with styles, but it turned out to be a mess.

We put together about fifty screens and quickly found common features in elements that used to look different.

In total, we drew about 70 screens, that covered most scenarios. Here are some of them:

Design system

In parallel with the work on the screens, we assembled a design system based on Material Design. And to make it useful for developers, we took into account their comments and added the missing information.

Made in 2019 in 5 weeks
