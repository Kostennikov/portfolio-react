1C Videocalls

The work looks juicier on a big screen

1C is one of the biggest Russian software companies. Some are familiar with the company either personally or through stereotypes (some of which are true).

Recently, 1C has introduced chats and video calls, which are used by many employees and customers of the company. They have long been accustomed to the program and its features, so it was important to make a design familiar to 1C users, but at the same time fresh, convenient and non-conflicting with other apps for calls.

Special thanks to the guys from 1C for their active participation and courage.

Grid without unnecessary emptiness
You can scroll cropped images

One of the features is a different approach to grid formation.Participants without a camera do not occupy space in the center of the screen, but are located on top in neat blocks. Only participants with theirs cameras on are displayed in the main grid.

Another feauture — emoji for participants without a camera.

The interface looks even neater, livelier and more friendly with it. A random emoji is assigned by default, but one can change it. The app will remember the choice and will assign it again next time.

Screen sharing

One of the annoying things in screen sharing is app interface. In Zoom, for example, there's a huge panel, that has to be constantly moved around the screen.

We made it easier — hid it by default and made it visible by hovering the cursor over the upper border of the screen. And so that users don’t get lost, when screen sharing starts, the bar hangs for a few seconds and then gently hides.
